Telescope Automation Project

Automate Telescope with DIY kit and control your telescope with a click
Point, Track or Find any star or galaxy

Project Description

Software powered by strong Database, along with user-friendly interface. Uniquely designed, easy-to-use and maintain electronics. Smartly designed 3D printable parts to fit any size of telescope without much hustle. Highly accurate maths at the core to make the tracking to get 0.0001 precision.

USP:

  • This automation kit can be attached to any size of same cateogory telescope
  • Large number of stars and deep-sky objects in the Database + Expandable Database
  • Can be configured to new gear-systems
